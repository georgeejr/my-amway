Registration

How to access Registration from the Home Page
  1. Register as an ABO

    First time Applicants can sign up for an ABO account by clicking on the ‘Register Here‘ button. This will take the Applicant to Step 1 of the registration process, to fill in their details.

Registration Process Overview

Applicant must successfully complete all 4 steps in order to register as an ABO

Step 1

Register as an ABO with Basic Info
  1. Citizenship

    Applicant must select their citizenship from the available options in the drop down list.

    *** To apply for the ABOship, an Applicant has to be either a permanent citizen or a resident of Malaysia.

  2. Age declaration above 18

    Applicant must declare if they are over 18 years old or not by clicking yes or no.

    *** To apply for the ABOship, an Applicant has to be 18 years and above.

  3. Marital Status

    Applicant must declare if their marital status by clicking either 'Single' or 'Married'.

  4. Sponsoring ABO details

    In order to apply for an ABOship, the Applicant must be sponsored by a current ABO. Applicant must declare if they have a sponsor by clicking either ‘Yes’ or ‘No’. If ‘Yes’, the Applicant will fill in their sponsor’s ABO details in Step 2. If 'No', the Applicant will be assigned a sponsor ABO by Amway.

    *** This assignment is a manual offline process.

  1. International Sponsorship Information

    Applicant must click the checkbox if they have an international sponsor. On selecting this checkbox, there will be two options to select from:

    International Sponsor - In case the Applicant has details about the international sponsoring ABO.

    Multiple Business  - In case the Applicant holds an Amway Business in another country.

  2. Link to PDFs

    There are two PDF links containing Amway business related content. Applicants can click these links to view the Business Reference Guide or Terms and Conditions (T&C).

  3. Confirm & Proceed

    Clicking on the 'Confirm and Proceed‘ button will take the Applicant to Step 2 of registration, provided all the fields have been completed correctly.

Step 2

Fill In Registration Details - Form displayed is based on input from Step 1 and Step 2
  1. First Name

    Applicant must fill in their first name.

  2. Surname

    Applicant must fill in their last name/ surname.

  3. Identity Number

    Applicant must fill in their unique identification number. First, select the type of identity card from the drop down list- New IC No(MyKad), Old Military No and Old IC No. Then fill in the identification number in the next box.

  4. Date of Birth

    Applicant must fill in their date of birth with the following format- date, month, year (DD/MM/YYYY). If the Applicant has added a new MyKad IC#, then the DOB will be entered by default however, it is still editable if they wish to change it.

    *** The age of Applicant must always be greater than 18 years from the date of birth entered.

  1. Race

    Applicant must select their racial group from the available options in the drop down list- Malay, Chinese, Indian or Other.

  2. Mobile Number

    Applicant must fill in their mobile number. A One Time Password will be sent to this number via text message for verification.

    *** Applicant must fill in either a mobile number or email address to receive the One Time Password (see 7).

  3. Email Address

    If the Applicant selects the check box, 'I do not have a mobile', then they must fill in their email address (eg. abcd@xyz.com). A One Time Password will be sent to this email for verification.

    *** Applicant must fill in either a mobile number or email address to receive the One Time Password (see 6)

  4. Spouse Details

    This section will only appear if the Applicant selected 'Married‘ as their 'Marital Status' in Step 1. The Applicant must fill in the following details about their spouse- First Name, Surname and Identity Number.

    *** To apply for the ABOship, an Applicant cannot have a spouse who is already an ABO

Address Registration
  1. Address Line 1

    Applicant must fill in the first line of their registered address.

  2. Address Line 2

    Applicant must fill in the second line of their registered address.

  3. Address Line 3

    Applicant must fill in the third line of their registered address.

  4. Postcode

    Applicant must fill in the postcode of their registered address.

  5. City

    Applicant must fill in the city of their registered address.

  6. State

    Applicant must select the state they live in from the drop down list.

Sponsor ABO Details
  1. ABO Sponsor Number

    If the Applicant selected 'I have a sponsoring ABO‘ in Step 1, this section will appear. Applicant must fill in the ABO Sponsor’s ID number.

  2. ABO Sponsor Name

    If the Applicant selected I have a sponsoring ABO ‘ in Step 1, this section will appear. Applicant must fill in the first three letters of their ABO Sponsor’s name and the full name will be generated from the database list.

  3. International ABO Number

    If the Applicant selected the checkbox for International Business in Step 1, this section appears. Applicant must fill in their International ABO Sponsor’s ID number.

  4. International ABO Name

    If the Applicant selected the checkbox for International Business in Step 1, this section appears. Applicant must fill in their International ABO Sponsor’s name.

  1. International ABO Country

    If the Applicant selected the checkbox for International Business in Step 1, this section appears. Applicant must select the country of their International ABO Sponsor from the drop down list.

  2. Checkbox

    Applicant needs to check the box 'I agree' to accept the International Business rule.

  3. Back

    Applicant can click ‘Back’ to return to the previous step of the registration process to change their details

  4. Submit My Details

    Clicking on the ‘Submit' button will take the Applicant to the One Time Password validation step of registration, provided all the fields have been completed correctly.

One Time Password Validation

Pop up after Step 2, verification sent to mobile number or email address.

  1. One Time Password Pop Up

    If all the fields have been filled in correctly then the One Time Password pop up will appear. A One Time Password is sent to either the mobile number or email address provided in Step 2.

  2. One Time Password Validation

    The Applicant must enter this One Time Password in the box and click on the 'Validate Now’ button to proceed. If the One Time Password is correct, the pop up closes and Step 3 of registration appears.

  3. Resend One Time Password Validation

    Applicant can reattempt to send the One Time Password to their mobile/email by clicking the 'Resend OTP' button.

Step 3

Confirm and Pay
  1. Information Summary

    This page displays the information provided by the Applicant in Step 1 and Step 2 for review before final submission.

  2. Edit Basic Info

    In case the Applicant needs to change their information in that section, they can click ‘edit’ and will be redirected to that section of the registration steps to change their information.

  3. Edit Registration Details

    In case the Applicant needs to change their information in that section , they can click ‘edit’ and will be redirected to that section of the registration steps to change their information.

  1. ABO Fees

    The applicant is displayed the ABO membership fees and is expected to make a payment.

  2. Checkbox

    Applicant needs to check the box 'I have read and agreed to the Terms & Conditions' to proceed to the payment page.

  3. Back

    Applicant can click ‘Back’ to return to the previous step of the registration process to change their details.

  4. Pay Now

    Clicking on the ‘Pay Now' button will redirect the Applicant to the payment page, provided all the fields have been completed correctly. This payment page is hosted on the payment gateway site.

Payment
 
  1. Choose Card

    Applicant must select a saved card from the dropdown list or click ‘Add a New Card’.

  2. Card Number

    Applicant must fill in their Card Number.

  3. Expiry Date

    Applicant must fill in the Expiry Date of their card with the following format- month and year (MM/YY).

  4. CVV

    Applicant must fill in the 3 digit code on the back on their card.

  5. Cardholder Name

    Applicant must fill in their name as it appears on their card.

  6. Checkbox

    ABO must check the box ‘Save this card for future use’ if they do not want to input their details next time they make a payment.

  7. Make Payment

    Clicking on the ‘Make Payment' button will redirect the Applicant to the next step of registration, provided all the fields have been completed correctly and the payment is successful. ABO must click ‘Cancel’ if they do not want to continue with the payment.

Step 4

Completion- Choose Password
 
  1. Password

    The ABO Applicant is required to choose a password for online login.

    *** Password must be minimum 6 characters and include at least 1 letter and 1 number.

  2. Re-enter Password

    The ABO Applicant is required to re-enter the password for confirmation.

    *** Password must be minimum 6 characters and include at least 1 letter and 1 number.

  3. Submit

    Clicking on the ‘Submit' button will take the Applicant to the final step of registration, provided the passwords match.

Completion Page
 
  1. My Account

    By clicking on 'My Account' the ABO is redirected to the respective page in a logged in state to update their details in the My Account section.

    *** Password must be minimum 6 characters and include at least 1 letter and 1 number.

  2. Shop

    By clicking on 'Shop' the ABO is redirected to the respective page to buy and get Amway products delivered.

    *** Password must be minimum 6 characters and include at least 1 letter and 1 number.

  3. Business Center

    By clicking on 'Business Center' the ABO is redirected to the respective page to manage their business.

  4. Knowledge

    By clicking on 'Knowledge' the ABO is redirected to the respective page to get training and resource materials.

  5. Summary

    Applicant will see a confirmation of their new password, summary of the details they provided and payment made.
