Voucher

How to Access Vouchers
  1. Hover Over Header

    After logging in, ABO must hover over their name in the header until the drop down list appears with the different sections of My Account.

  2. Vouchers

    ABO must click on ‘Vouchers’ to proceed to the Vouchers section.

Voucher Page:

Credit Vouchers

  1. Navigate My Account Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of My Account.

  2. Navigate Voucher Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of their Vouchers.

  3. Coupons

    This section displays the cash vouchers/credit notes issued to the ABO for any returned or cancelled orders.

    *** The list name must be unique within the country.

  4. Sort Coupons

    ABO can sort coupons by clicking the arrows on the filter.

Voucher Page:

Coupons

  1. Navigate My Account Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of My Account.

  2. Navigate Voucher Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of their Vouchers.

  3. Coupons

    This section displays the trade-in coupons issued to the ABO in exchange of any product (like e-Spring). ABO can use this coupon to buy the trade-in product for which the coupon was issued.

  4. Sort Coupons

    ABO can sort coupons by clicking the arrows on the filter.
