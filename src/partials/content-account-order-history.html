Order History

How to Access Order History
  • Hover Over Header

    After logging in, ABO must hover over their name in the header until the drop down list appears with the different sections of My Account.

  • Order History

    ABO must click on ‘Order History’ to proceed to the Order History section.

Open Orders
  • Navigate My Account Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of My Account.

  • Navigate Order History Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of their Order History.

  • Open Orders

    ABO can see all the details for their orders which have a pending or failed status.

All Orders
  • Navigate My Account Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of My Account.

  • Navigate Order History Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of their Order History.

  • Quick Search Orders

    ABO can search for specific order details using the Order ID number.

  • Select View

    ABO can filter which orders they want to view.

  • Show Orders by Date

    ABO can search for specific order details by selecting a Preset Month or a Date Range.

  • Search Filter

    ABO can filter which orders they want to view based on Order Type and Order Channel.

  • Show Orders

    Clicking on the ‘Show Orders’ button will allow the ABO to view the list of orders based on the filters set above. The generated order list will appear below.

Order List View
  • Navigate My Account Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of My Account.

  • Sort Orders

    ABO can sort orders by clicking the arrows on the filter.

  • Go to Page

    Entering a page number and clicking on the ‘Go’ button will allow the ABO to skip to a specific page in the orders list.

  • Navigate Order Pages

    ABO can click on pages to view more orders.

Detailed Orders View
Open Preview
  • Navigate My Account Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of My Account.

  • Expand Order Details

    Clicking on the expand icon/arrow on the rightmost corner of each order row will expand the order preview below.
