Address Book

How to Access Vouchers
  • Hover Over Header

    After logging in, ABO must hover over their name in the header until the drop down list appears with the different sections of My Account.

  • Address Book

    ABO must click on ‘Address Book’ to proceed to the Address Book section.

Address Book Page:

Landing

  • Navigate My Account Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of My Account.

  • My Registered Address

    This section displays the personal address provided by the ABO during the registration process

  • My Shipping Address

    This section displays the ABO’s shipping addresses. ABO can add new shipping address, modify address, delete address, search address from the address book.

  • Navigate Address Book

    ABO can click on pages to view more addresses.

  • Go to Page

    Entering a page number and clicking on the ‘Go’ button will allow the ABO to skip to a specific page in the address book.

Step 1

How to Add a New Address
  • Navigate My Account Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of My Account.

  • Add New Address

    Clicking on the ‘Add New Address’ button will allow the ABO to create a new shipping address.

Step 2

How to Add a New Address
  • Navigate My Account Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of My Account.

  • Fill New Address

    ABO is prompted  to enter new address in the fields provided.

  • Add New Address

    Clicking on the ‘Save’ button will allow the ABO to create a new shipping address.
