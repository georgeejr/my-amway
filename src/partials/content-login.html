Login

How to Login
  1. Login as an ABO

    ABO must log in to their account by filling in their username and password and clicking on the ‘Submit‘ button. This will take the ABO into their account.

  2. Remember Me Checkbox

    ABO must check the box ‘Remember Me’ if they want the system to remember their username and password details for future login attempts.

  3. Forgot Login Details

    Clicking on this link will redirect user to ‘Forgot Password’ where they can request for their details to be sent to them.

How to Logout
  1. Hover Over Header

    After logging in, ABO must hover over their name in the header until the drop down list appears with the different sections of My Account.

  2. Logout

    ABO must click on ‘Logout’ in order to logout of their session.
