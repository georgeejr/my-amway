How to access Profile
  • Hover Over Header

    After logging in, ABO must hover over their name in the header until the drop down list appears with the different sections of My Account.

  • Profile

    ABO must click on ‘Profile’ to proceed to the Profile section.

  • Forgot Login Details

    Clicking on this link will redirect user to ‘Forgot Password’ where they can request for their details to be sent to them.
