Profile

How to access Profile
  1. Hover Over Header

    After logging in, ABO must hover over their name in the header until the drop down list appears with the different sections of My Account.

  2. Profile

    ABO must click on ‘Profile’ to proceed to the Profile section.

Primary ABO Details
  1. Navigate My Account Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of My Account.

  2. Navigate Profile Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of their Profile.

  3. Primary ABO Details

    This section contains the profile information for the Primary ABO (main applicant).

    *** Details are view only and cannot be changed or updated, except photo.

  4. Personal Details

    This section displays the personal information provided by the ABO during the registration process.

    *** Only some fields are editable.

  5. Contact Details

    This section displays the contact information provided by the ABO during the registration process.

    *** These fields are editable.

Co-Applicant / Spouse Details
  1. Navigate My Account Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of My Account.

  2. Navigate Profile Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of their Profile.

  3. Personal Details

    This section displays personal information about the spouse, if available.

    *** Only some fields are editable.

  4. Contact Details

    This section displays contact information for the spouse.

    *** Only some fields are editable.

  5. Confidentiality Checkbox

    ABO needs to check the box ‘'I want my spouse information to remain confidential’ if they do not want to share their spouse information with Amway.

My Recognition
  1. Navigate My Account Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of My Account.

  2. Navigate Profile Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of their Profile.

  3. Recognition

    This section will contain information about the ABO which shall be used by Amway for Recognition purposes.

  4. T&Cs Checkbox

    ABO needs to check the box ‘'I have read and agreed to the Terms & Conditions’ to save their name.

  5. Recognition Name

    ABO can provide their 'Recognition Name' here.

    *** Name must be unique and not used by any other ABO.

  6. Save Recognition

    Clicking on the ‘Save’ button will allow the ABO to save changes made to their Recognition Name.
