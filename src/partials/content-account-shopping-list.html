Shopping List

How to Access Shopping List
  • Hover Over Header

    After logging in, ABO must hover over their name in the header until the drop down list appears with the different sections of My Account.

  • Shopping List

    ABO must click on ‘Shopping List’ to proceed to the Shopping List section.

Shopping List Page
  • Navigate My Account Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of My Account.

  • Shopping List Options

    ABO can see all shopping lists for the account and can select default/ favourite list, edit name or delete lists.

Shopping List Page

View Individual Shopping List

  • Navigate My Account Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of My Account.

  • Expand List Details

    Clicking on the expand icon/arrow on the rightmost corner of each shopping list row will expand the details below.

  • Shopping List Options

    ABO can click ‘Select All’ to perform collective operations on all products from the shopping list. ABO can click ‘Move to Cart’ to copy selected products from the shopping list to the shopping cart. ABO can click ‘Delete’ to delete products from the list.

  • Total PV / BV

    ABO can view the total PV/BV for all the products in the shopping list and the total distributor price of all the products in the shopping list.

Step 1

How to Add a New Shopping List
  • Navigate My Account Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of My Account.

  • Add New List

    Clicking on the ‘Add New List’ button will allow the ABO to create a new shopping list.

Step 2

How to Add a New Shopping List
  • Navigate My Account Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of My Account.

  • Add List Name

    ABO is prompted  to enter the name of the new list.

    *** The list name must be unique within the country.

  • Add New List

    Clicking on the ‘Add’ button will allow the ABO to create a new shopping list with the chosen name.
