Resignation

How to access Resignation section from the Home Page
  1. Resign as an ABO

    ABO must log in to their account by filling in their username and password and clicking on the ‘Submit‘ button. This will take the ABO into their account.

Goes into Account Settings
  1. Hover Over Header

    After logging in, ABO must hover over their name in the header until the drop down list appears with the different sections of My Account.

  2. Logout

    ABO must click on ‘Logout’ in order to logout of their session.

Registration Process Overview

Applicant must successfully complete all 5 steps in order to resign as an ABO

Step 1

ABO goes to ‘Resignation’ section
  1. Resignation Section

    ABO must scroll down to the ‘Resignation’ section of their Account Settings.

Step 2

ABO agrees to T&Cs and submits request
  1. Resignation Checkbox

    ABO needs to check the box ‘I agree to above policies and confirm to terminate my ABOship‘ in order to proceed with their resignation.

  2. Submit Resignation

    Clicking on the ‘Submit My Resignation’ button will allow the ABO to request for resignation.

Step 3

Payment page
  1. One Time Password Pop Up

    A One Time Password pop up will appear to confirm the Resignation submission. A One Time Password is sent to either the mobile number or email address provided in ABO’s account.

  2. One Time Password Validation

    The ABO must enter this One Time Password in the box and click on the ‘Confirm’ button to proceed. If the One Time Password is correct, the pop up closes and a resignation request is made.

  3. Resend One Time Password Validation

    ABO can reattempt to send the One Time Password to their mobile/email by clicking the 'Resend OTP' button.
