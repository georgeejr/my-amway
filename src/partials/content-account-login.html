How to access My Account from The Home Page
  • Login as an ABO

    ABO must log in to their account by filling in their username and password and clicking on the ‘Submit‘ button. This will take the ABO into their account where they can access the My Account section.

Goes into Account Settings
  • Hover Over Header

    ABO must hover over their name in the header until the drop down list appears with the different sections of My Account.

  • Navigate My Account Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of My Account.
