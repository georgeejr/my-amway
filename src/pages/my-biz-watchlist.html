MyBiz Watchlist

How to access Watchlist
  1. Access Watchlist

    ABO must click on the menu icon at the top left hand corner to navigate the different MyBiz sections. ABO must click on ‘Watchlist’ to proceed to view Watchlist section.

Watchlist Page:
Landing
  1. Navigate MyBiz

    ABO can click on the icons to access different sections of MyBiz.

  2. Watchlist

    In this section the ABO can view a custom list of their Downlines which they have curated. This allows ABOs to track certain Downlines more closely.

  3. List View

    Only a list view is available for the Watchlist page. ABO can customize report columns by selecting the icon on the right hand side

    ***Tree View will be disabled here.

  4. Control Menu

    Downlines will appear as they do in the Action Reports and LOS. ABO can take action on any row in the list by clicking on the Control Menu on the right hand side.

  5. Add to Watchlist

    ABO can add a Downline to their Watchlist by clicking the eye icon on the left side of that row.
