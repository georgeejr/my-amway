Payment Details

How to Access Payment Details
  1. Hover Over Header

    After logging in, ABO must hover over their name in the header until the drop down list appears with the different sections of My Account.

  2. Payment Details

    ABO must click on ‘Payment Details’ to proceed to the Payment Details section.

Payment Details Page:
Manage Your Card
  1. Navigate My Account Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of My Account.

  2. Navigate Payment Details Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of their Payment Details.

  3. My Card

    ABO can see their saved payment cards in this section.

    *** Card details generated from payment gateway.

  4. Manage Your Card

    Clicking on ‘Delete’ will allow ABO to delete a saved card. Clicking on ‘Edit’ will allow the ABO to edit a card nick name.

    *** Nick name must be maximum 20 characters.

Payment Details Page:
Bank Accounts
  1. Navigate My Account Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of My Account.

  2. Navigate Payment Details Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of their Payment Details.

  3. Account Details

    ABO can view the Bank Account details where they will receive the bonus.

    *** Card details generated from payment gateway.
