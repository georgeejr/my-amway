ITCCAS- Intent to Continue Cum Amagram Subscription

How to access ITCCAS
  1. ITCCAS

    Clicking on the ‘ITCCAS’ icon in the quick links bar will redirect the ABO to the ITCCAS Group Renewal page of the Business Center.

ITCCAS Page:
ITCCAS Inquiry
  1. Navigate Business Center

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of Business Center.

  2. ITCCAS

    ABO can view the list and details of group renewals done in the past and their payment status.

  3. Retry Payment

    Clicking on an ‘Unprocessed’ ITCCAS row will redirect ABO to retry payment for any failed group renewal transactions.

ITCCAS Page:
ITCCAS Retry Payment
  1. Navigate Business Center

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of Business Center.

  2. Payment Details

    ABO can view the details of failed group renewal transaction.

  3. Make Payment

    ABO can retry payment for failed transaction by clicking on ‘Make Payment Now’ button.

  4. Edit Payment

    ABO can retry payment for failed transaction by clicking on ‘Make Payment Now’ button.

Step1:
How to renew Amagram for group renewal
  1. Navigate Business Center

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of Business Center.

  2. Search Downlines

    ABO can search for downlines they want to select for group renewal by filling field and clicking enter. ABO can search downlines by ABO ID or ABO Name.

  3. Checkbox

    ABO can either select individual downlines for renewal by checking the box next to their ABO number or ABO can check the ‘Select All’ box to renew all downlines.

  4. Choose Subscription

    ABO can choose between eAmagram (RM 30) and Amagram (RM 43) subscription types.

  5. Cancel

    To cancel group renewal ABO must click ‘Cancel’ button and select ‘Yes’ on confirmation pop up.

  6. Add Downlines to ITCCAS

    After selecting downlines for group renewal, ABO must click on ‘Add Downlines to ITCCAS’ button to confirm and proceed to next step of group renewal.

Step2:
How to renew Amagram for group renewal
  1. Navigate Business Center

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of Business Center.

  2. ITCCAS Subscription

    ABO can view subscription type selected on previous page. Clicking on ‘Edit’ button will redirect ABO to Page 1 of ITCCAS to change their subscription type.

  3. Continue Downline Distributorship

    ABO can view details of downlines selected for group renewal. Clicking on ‘Edit’ button will redirect ABO to Page 1 of ITCCAS to change their selected downlines.

  4. Payment Options

    AIn order to proceed with group renewal, ABO must select E-banking or Visa/Mastercard from the payment options.

    ***This is a mandatory field.

  5. ITCCAS Agreement

    This section contains terms and conditions for ITCCAS. ABO can click the Download icon to download PDF of T&Cs in chosen language. ABO must select "I accept" check box to agree to T&Cs in order to proceed with group renewal.

  6. Cancel

    To cancel group renewal ABO must click ‘Cancel’ button and select ‘Yes’ on confirmation pop up.

  7. Submit

    In order to complete group renewal ABO must click on ‘Submit’ button to be proceed to payment page.
