How to Access

Step 1: How to access Knowledge Center (before login)
  1. Access Knowledge Center

    ABO must click the tab ‘Knowledge’ in the navigation menu to proceed to the Knowledge Center Landing Page.

Step 2: How to access Knowledge Center (before login)
  1. Landing Page Before Login

    When the ABO is not logged in, this landing page appears after clicking on the Knowledge tab in the navigation menu.

  2. Login

    ABO can log in to their account by filling in their username and password and clicking on the ‘Submit‘ button. This will take the ABO into the Knowledge Center.

Knowledge Center Page: After Login Landing
  1. Landing Page After Login

    When the ABO is already logged in, they are redirected to this Knowledge Center Landing Page after clicking on the Knowledge tab in the navigation menu.

  2. eLearning(LMS)

    ABO can view Courses, Downloads and Videos in this section. Clicking on ‘View More’ on top right corner or on the tiles will redirect ABO to eLearning page.

  3. Latest Videos

    ABO can view the latest videos for products in this section.

  4. Product Information

    ABO can view and download PDF files with information about products in this section.

  1. Training Events

    ABO can view and browse Upcoming Workshops, Register for training events and make an Appointment inquiry/booking in this section.

  2. Digital Hotspot

    ABO can discover Amway apps, microsites and other digital resources in this section.

  3. Business Information

    ABO can view and download PDF files with information about their business and Amway policies in this section.
