Product Warranty

How to access Product Warranty
  1. Knowledge Tab

    ABO must hover over ‘Knowledge’ tab in the header until the drop down list appears with the different sections.

  2. Product Warranty

    ABO must click on ‘Product Warranty Registration’ to proceed to the Product Warranty section

Product Warranty Page:
My Product
  1. Navigate Business Center

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of Business Center.

  2. Print/ Download

    ABO can 'Print' and 'Download' the page in report format by clicking these buttons in the top right.

  3. Navigate Product Warranty Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections. ABO can manage the warranty for brand products for them self in My Product.

  4. Quick Access

    ABO can filter the registration products by selecting a Brand from the drop down list. ABO can quickly register the product by typing in the Serial Number and clicking the ‘Add’ button to proceed to the registration form. Or ABO can search for the serial number entered by clicking the ‘Search’ button, if it already exists in the list.

  5. Filters

    ABO can filter results using the drop down list: All Products, Registered Products and Unregistered Products or by entering the Date of Purchase.

  6. Un-Registered Products

    ABO can view a list of un-registered products. Clicking on ‘Register’ button on right hand side will redirect ABO to Registration form.

  7. Registered Products

    ABO can view a list of registered products. Clicking on ‘View’ button on far right hand side will allow the ABO to view the warranty details. Clicking on ‘Edit’ button on right hand side opens the form to edit warranty details.

  8. Navigate Pages

    ABO can click on pages on the left side to view more or enter a page number and click the ‘Go’ button to skip to a specific page.

Step1:
How to Register a New Product Warranty
  1. Register New Product Warranty

    Clicking the ‘Register’ button on right hand side will redirect ABO to Registration form.

Step2:
How to register a new product warranty
  1. General Information

    ABO can optionally fill in ‘Register To’ if the product warranty is being registered for a downline. ABO must fill in their ABO Number and the Name of the customer to whom the product is sold.

  2. Contact Details

    ABO must fill in their contact details.
    ***(* means mandatory)

  3. Product Details

    ABO must fill in product details. ***Installation Date cannot be later than 1 year after purchase date.)

  4. Checkbox

    ABO must select "I accept" check box to agree to T&Cs in order to proceed to register a new product warranty.

  5. Submit

    In order to register a new product warranty, ABO must click on ‘Submit’ button to proceed.

  6. Reset

    To reset fields for product warranty, ABO must click ‘Reset’ button.
