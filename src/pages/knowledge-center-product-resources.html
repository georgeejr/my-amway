Product Resources

How to access Product Resources
  1. Access Product Resources

    ABO must click the section ‘Product Information’ on the Knowledge Center landing page to proceed.

Nutrition & Wellness Page
Videos
  1. Navigate Knowledge Center

    ABO must click the section ‘Product Information’ on the Knowledge Center landing page to proceed.

  2. Sort Videos

    ABO can sort videos by date using the dropdown list on the right or by selecting a filter from the drop down list on the left.

  3. Navigate Nutrition & Wellness Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections.

  4. View Videos

    ABO can choose to view the videos in a list or a grid by clicking the icons on the right hand side.

  1. Watch Videos

    ABO can watch videos by clicking on play icon.

  2. Navigate Video Pages

    ABO can click on pages to view more videos.

  3. Go to Page

    Entering a page number and clicking on the ‘Go’ button will allow the ABO to skip to a specific page in the videos list.

  2. Sort Content

  4. View Content

    ABO can choose to view the content in a list or a grid by clicking the icons on the right hand side.

  1. Download Content

    ABO can download content in a PDF file by clicking ‘Download’.

  2. Preview Content

    ABO can click on the title or image to enlarge and preview the content.
