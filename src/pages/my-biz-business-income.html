MyBiz My Business Income

How to access My Business Income
  1. Access My Business Income

    ABO must click on the menu icon at the top left hand corner to navigate the different MyBiz sections. ABO must click on ‘My Business Income’ to proceed to view My Business Income section which gives an overview of Bonus income for the current year and previous year.

My Business Income Page:
Landing
  1. Navigate MyBiz

    ABO can click on the icons to access different sections of MyBiz.

  2. Choose Year

    ABO can toggle between the Current and Previous Performance Year by clicking on the left and right arrow icons.

  3. Graph

    ABO can see an overview of their Bonus income with the proportion of their earnings shown by Bonus type.

  4. Monthly Breakdown

    ABO can view the Monthly Breakdown of these bonuses by clicking on the row they want to view in detail.

  5. Total

    ABO can view the total monthly breakdown by clicking here.

My Business Income Page:
Monthly Breakdown
  1. Monthly Breakdown

    After clicking on a Bonus in the chart, ABO will proceed to the Monthly Breakdown Page for that Bonus. ABO can switch between bonuses by selecting from the list.

  2. View Statement

    ABO can click on a specific month to view a statement page for available bonuses.

  3. Graph

    ABO can view the Monthly Breakdown of these bonuses in this graph.

My Business Income Page:
Statement (ONLY for ILB)
  1. Statement

    After clicking on a specific month, ABO can view detailed Bonus Statement page.

    ***Only available for International Leadership Bonus.

My Business Income Page:
ILB Generators
  1. ILB Generators

    When viewing International Leadership Bonus, clicking a Month will take ABO to the ILB Generators Page. ABO can see who generated ILB and where they are in the business.

  2. Generator Indication

    These symbols indicate who generated, got none, got some, and got all of the ILB available.

  3. Choose Period

    ABO must select a date from the dropdown list to view ILB Generators from that chosen month.

  4. Income Flow

    Clicking the ‘Income Flow’ button for a Generator will expand that section to show the full flow of the Bonus to you from that Downline.
