MyBiz Dashboard

How to log in to MyBiz
  1. Select Country

    ABO must click on the menu icon at the top left hand corner to navigate the different MyBiz sections. ABO must click on ‘Dashboard’ to proceed to view Dashboard section.

Dashboard Page:
Video
  1. Welcome Banner

    After first Log In, ABO will see a Welcome banner on the Dashboard with the option to view a quick informative video about the functionalities of MyBiz.

  2. Play Video

    ABO must click on ‘Play Video’ button in the banner to expand the video to view.

Dashboard Page:
Landing
  1. Navigate MyBiz

    ABO can click on the icons to access different sections of MyBiz.

  2. Monthly Performance Metrics

    In this section the ABO can view their current monthly performance data.

  3. Key Performance Indicators

    In this section the ABO can view their KPI data. These are real-time indicators that provide deeper insights to business performance.

  4. Action Reports

    ABO can see who is new to their group- the left number is how many ABOs joined this Month, and the right number is new ABOs they have not viewed yet on MyBiz. ABO can also click Who’s Close Report to see a list of downlines who are close to their next Performance Bonus Level.

  5. Higher Award Tracking

    ABO can view how many downlines are tracking qualifications this year in their group and how many FAA Points they have collected in the current Performance Year.

    *** Must qualify to see this section in the Dashboard

  6. Performance History

    ABO can see a 12 month history of their Volume production and Sponsoring. In both graphs, the lines and dots will show Personal contribution, and the Bars will show Group totals for the months.

    *** Must scroll horizontally to view other months not in view.

Dashboard Page:
KPIs continued
  1. Sponsored

    The number of ABOs personally sponsored in the group.

  2. Personal Orders

    The number of Orders ABO has personally placed this Performance Period.

  3. Qualified Downlines

    The number of Qualified Downlines ABO has this month.

  4. Total ABOs

    The Total number of ABOs in personal Platinum group.

    *** Includes Platinums in your group but excludes those Platinums’ groups below them.

  5. Group Orders

    The total number of Orders by group.

  6. New

    The number of New ABOs in Primary Market Group this Performance Period.

Dashboard Page:
Contextual Help
  1. Contextual Help

    In order to further understand the Performance metrics, ABO can click on a metric and a Contextual Help overlay will appear with a detailed definition of the metric.

  2. Contextual Help

    More information is also available for the other Dashboard sections. ABO can click on the ‘i’ icon to view a detailed summary of the section.

How to search LOS tree
  1. Search LOS tree

    ABO can search their LOS tree for downlines by clicking on the search icon and typing in either the ABO name or ABO number they are searching for. ABO can then select an ABO from the search results to view LOS page with downline information.

Step1:
How to select performance period
  1. Change Performance Period

    ABO can choose the Performance Period data they want to see in the Dashboard by clicking on the date icon on the right hand side.

Step2:
How to select performance period
  1. Choose Period

    ABO must select a date from the dropdown list to view data from that chosen month.

    ***Changing the Performance Period will only after the Performance Data and KPIs in the dashboard, the other data will remain unchanged

Step1:
How to update profile photo
  1. Change Profile Photo

    ABO can add or change their profile photo by clicking ‘Edit’ in the Monthly Performance section of the MyBiz Dashboard.

Step2:
How to update profile photo
  1. Choose Photo

    An overlay will appear to Add a Profile Picture. ABO much click on ‘Choose Photo’ to select a photo from exiting pictures or a take a new photo.

  2. Save Photo

    ABO must click ‘Save’ button to update new photo or click on ‘Cancel’ button to keep original photo.

Step1:
How to select and view other businesses
  1. Select Business

    ABO can select any of their businesses and view that data in the Dashboard. ABO must click on ‘Select Business’ in the header to open the list of businesses.

Step2:
How to select and view other businesses
  1. Choose Business

    Once the ABO has opened the list of businesses, they can scroll down to find the business they want to view. Clicking on the row of the business will allow the ABO to view that business data in the Dashboard.

    ***Primary Business and all #2s appear in the list.
