Online Award Reporting (Only Platinum & Above)

How to access Online Award Reporting
  1. Online Awards

    Clicking on ‘Online Award Reporting’ in the Platinum and Above dashboard will redirect the ABO to Online Award section.

How to Nominate an Award Candidate
  1. Navigate Business Center

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of Business Center.

  2. Print/ Download

    ABO can 'Print' and 'Download' the page in report format by clicking these buttons in the top right.

  3. Nominate Candidate

    ABO must select an Award Type from the drop down list and fill in the nominated downlines’ ABO number in the field.

  4. Delete

    If ABO wants to nominate less than 5 candidates they can delete a row by clicking on ‘Delete’ icon on right hand side.

  5. Add More

    By default, 5 nomination fields will appear in the form. ABO can add more by filling a number in the field and clicking on ‘Add’ button to nominate more downlines.

  6. Checkbox

    ABO must select "I accept" check box to agree to T&Cs in order to proceed to nominate award candidates.

  7. Submit

    In order to complete award nominations request, ABO must click on ‘Submit’ button and confirm ‘Yes’ on pop up to proceed.

  8. Reset

    To reset fields for award candidate nomination, ABO must click ‘Reset’ button.
