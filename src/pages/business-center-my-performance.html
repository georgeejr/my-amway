Business Center

How to access Business Reports
  1. My Performance

    Clicking on the different sections of ‘My Performance’ in the Platinum and Above dashboard will redirect the ABO to My Performance section.

My Performance Page:
My ALS Points
  1. Navigate Business Center

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of Business Center.

  2. Print/ Download

    ABO can 'Print' and 'Download' the page in report format by clicking these buttons in the top right.

  3. ALS Points

    In this section the ABO can view their ALS points for the last performance year. They can view the total and the points broken down by month.

  4. Points Target

    ABO can see the number of points they need to reach their next target.

My Performance Page:
My GIP Progress
  1. Navigate Business Center

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of Business Center.

  2. Print/ Download-

    ABO can 'Print' and 'Download' the page in report format by clicking these buttons in the top right.

  3. GIP Progress

    In this section the ABO can view their GIP Progress for the current performance year.

    ***GIP Progress is measured in terms of SQ Bonus.

  4. SQ Bonus

    ABO can see details of their SQ Bonus in this section and a break down of their SQ Volume.

  5. Emerald ALS Program

    ABO can see details of their Emerald ALS Program in this section.

  6. Expand Sections

    ABO can view more sections by clicking on the expand icon/arrow on the rightmost corner.

  7. Emerald/Diamond Growth Incentive

    ABO can see details of their Emerald/ Diamond Growth Incentive in this section.

  8. Download

    This section will contain links to download various PDFs related to this section.

My Performance Page:
Platinum Re-qualification
  1. Navigate Business Center

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of Business Center.

  2. Print/ Download

    ABO can 'Print' and 'Download' the page in report format by clicking these buttons in the top right.

  3. Platinum Re-qualification

    This section displays Platinum re-qualification criteria for the ABO and the number of months ABO has achieved within the financial year.

Step1:
My ALS Calculator
  1. Navigate Business Center

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of Business Center.

  2. Print/ Download

    ABO can 'Print' and 'Download' the page in report format by clicking these buttons in the top right. ABO can also download the offline calculator as an HTML page to use offline anytime.

  3. ALS Calculator

    In this section the ABO can calculate an estimate of their ALS Points in order to plan their business progress.

    ***The calculation is an approximation only.

  4. How to Calculate

    ABO needs to enter values in the fields provided and select a country to calculate ALS Points.

  5. Submit

    In order to generate ALS calculation, ABO must click on ‘Calculate’ button to proceed

  6. Reset

    Clicking on ‘Order History’ will redirect the ABO to the Order History section of My Account.

  7. Health & Beauty Assessment

    To reset fields for ALS Calculator, ABO must click ‘Reset’ button.

Step2:
My ALS Calculator
  1. Edit

    ABO can fill in fields with different data and click ‘OK’ button at the bottom of the page to re-calculate their ALS Points.

  2. Results

    In this section the ABO can view their total ALS points calculated.

  3. Reset

    To reset fields for ALS Calculator, ABO must click ‘Reset’ button.

  4. Download Calculator

    ABO can download the offline calculator as an HTML page to use offline anytime.
