MyBiz Line of Sponsorship (LOS)

How to access LOS
  1. Access LOS

    ABO must click on the menu icon at the top left hand corner to navigate the different MyBiz sections. ABO must click on ‘Line of Sponsorship’ to proceed to view Line of Sponsorship section.

LOS Page:
Landing (List View)
  1. Navigate MyBiz

    ABO can click on the icons to access different sections of MyBiz.

  2. Choose Period

    ABO must select a date from the dropdown list to view LOS data from that chosen month.

  3. Expand Details

    Clicking on the expand icon/arrow ‘Expand All’ on the left will expand full LOS. ABO can just expand one Downline details by clicking on the expand icon/arrow on the left of each row.

    ***Only available when looking at the In Market

  4. Level Indicator

    ABO will land on LOS page in List View. Downlines are arranged top down showing highest total ABOs on top. The ascending numbers indicate the Downlines’ level in the business.

    ***Michael Mann is at the third level of this business.

  5. View

    ABO can select the right hand icon for a List View or the center icon to see a Tree View of their LOS. ABO can customize LOS list columns by selecting the icon on the right hand side.

  6. Watchlist

    ABO can add a Downline to their Watchlist by clicking the eye icon on the left side.

  7. Choose Business LOS

    ABO must choose between Primary In Market or International Business LOS. Switching between the two will automatically update the LOS with the appropriate data.

  8. Control Menu

    ABO can take action on any Downline row in the list by clicking on the Control Menu icon on the right hand side.

LOS Page:
Landing (Tree View)
  1. Navigate MyBiz

    ABO can click on the icons to access different sections of MyBiz.

  2. Choose Business LOS

    ABO must choose between Primary In Market or International Business LOS. Switching between the two will automatically update the LOS Tree with the appropriate data.

  3. Choose Period

    ABO must select a date from the dropdown list to view LOS data from that chosen month.

  4. View

    ABO can select the right hand icon for a List View or the center icon to see a Tree View of their LOS. ABO can choose to view either the ABO Name or ABO Number in Tree View by selecting the icon on the right hand side. ABO must select their preferred option and click ‘Apply’ button to save changes.

  5. Select Downline

    ABO can click on any Downline to highlight them and zoom in on their details. ABO can take action on any Downline in the Tree by clicking on the Control Menu icon on the bottom right corner of the selected Downline.

  6. Colour Key

    ABO can use the colour key at the bottom to decode the Downline’s colour and Bonus level from the LOS Tree.

How to switch View
  1. Switch View

    ABO can select the right hand icon for a List View or the center icon to see a Tree View of their LOS.

How to change Columns
  1. Control Menu

    ABO can change the Columns in View on the LOS List by clicking on the Control Menu icon on the right hand side.

  2. Select Columns

    ABO must drag and drop the Columns in the list on the right hand side and put them in the order they want to see.

    ***Columns currently in view will look white with a blue stripe next to them. ABO can select from an extensive list of columns below.

  3. Apply

    To complete the configuration of Columns in LOS List, ABO must click ‘Apply’ button on the right hand side.

Step1:
How to View Profile
  1. View Profile

    ABO must click on the Control Menu icon on the right hand side of the Downline row they want to see, then select ‘View Profile’ to proceed to profile.

Step2:
How to View Profile
  1. Slideout

    After clicking ‘View Profile’ a slideout will appear with the Profile information for that Downline.

  2. Select Downline

    ABO can select another Downline while the slideout Profile is open and the informationwill automatically update for that Downline.

  3. View Performance

    ABO can switch between the Profile and Performance sections of the Downlines using these toggles.

  4. Close

    ABO can exit the View Profile section by clicking the X icon on the top right hand corner.

How to Expand Group
  1. Control Menu

    ABO must click on the Control Menu icon on the right hand side of the Downline row they want to see.

  2. Expand Group

    ABO must select ‘Expand Group’ in the Control Menu to expand the entire group of that ABO.

How to Isolate
  1. Control Menu

    ABO must click on the Control Menu icon on the right hand side of the Downline row they want to see.

  2. Isolate

    ABO must select ‘Isolate’ in the Control Menu pull the selected Downline to the top of the LOS List.

  3. Isolated Downline

    The #1 position on the LOS List will show the details of the Isolated Downline. ABO can trace LOS from the breadcrumb on the top left hand side.

Step1:
How to View Performance
  1. View Performance

    ABO must click on the Control Menu icon on the right hand side of the Downline row they want to see, then select ‘View Performance’ to proceed.

Step2:
How to View Performance
  1. Slideout

    After clicking ‘View Performance’ a slideout will appear with the Performance information for that Downline, which appears as a mini Dashboard.

  2. Select Downline

    ABO can select another Downline while the slideout is open and the Performance information will automatically update for that Downline.

  3. View Profile

    ABO can switch between the Profile and Performance sections of the Downlines using these toggles.

  4. Close

    ABO can exit the View Performance section by clicking the X icon on the top right hand corner.

Step1:
How to View Performance
  1. Add to Watchlist

    ABO can add a Downline to their Watchlist by clicking the eye icon on the left side while still remaining on the LOS Page. If the action was successful, the Watchlist indicator will appear.

  2. Remove from Watchlist

    ABO can remove any Downline from their Watchlist by clicking on the Control Menu icon on the right hand side.
