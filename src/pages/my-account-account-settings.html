Account Settings

How to Access Account Settings
  • Hover Over Header

    After logging in, ABO must hover over their name in the header until the drop down list appears with the different sections of My Account.

  • Account Settings

    ABO must click on ‘Account Settings’ to proceed to the Account Settings section.

Account Settings Page:
Landing
  • Navigate My Account Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of My Account.

  • Change/Update Username

    In this section, ABO can change or update their username for login.

  • Change/Update Password

    In this section, ABO can change or update their password for login.

  • Resignation

    In this section, ABO can terminate their ABO membership.

How to Change/Update Username
  • Navigate My Account Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of My Account.

  • Change/Update Username

    Clicking on ‘Change’ will allow the ABO to change/update their username to use for login instead of their ABO ID.

    *** New username must be between 6-20 characters long and contain one letter of the alphabet.

  • Save Changes

    Clicking on the ‘Save’ button will allow the ABO to save changes to their username.

How to Change/Update Password
  • Navigate My Account Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of My Account.

  • Change/Update Username

    ABO must enter their old password and confirm their new password twice in order to change.

    *** New password must be between 8-16 characters and a combination of letters and numbers.

  • Save Changes

    Clicking on the ‘Save’ button will allow the ABO to save changes to their username.
