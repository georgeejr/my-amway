Business Reports

How to access Business Reports
  1. Option 1

    Clicking on the ‘Business Report’ icon in the quick links bar will redirect the ABO to the Business Report section of the Business Center.

  2. Option 2

    Clicking on specific reports in Business Reports section of Dashboard will redirect ABO to the Business Reports section of the Business Center.

Business Report Page:
Platinum Activity Report
  1. Navigate Business Center

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of Business Center.

  2. Print/ Download

    ABO can 'Print' and 'Download' the page in report format by clicking these buttons in the top right.

  3. Platinum Activity Report

    This report is to notify Platinum ABO on the activity in the previous month.

  4. Renewed ABOs

    ABO can see details of ABOs who have renewed during the last month.

  5. Non-renewed ABOs

    ABO can see details of ABOs whose ABOship has expired during the last month.

  6. Resigned ABOs

    ABO can see details of ABOs who have resigned during the last month.

  7. Expand ABO Details

    ABO can view more details by clicking on the expand icon/arrow on the rightmost corner.

Business Report Page:
Intent to Continue Report
  1. Navigate Business Center

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of Business Center.

  2. Print/ Download

    ABO can 'Print' and 'Download' the page in report format by clicking these buttons in the top right.

  3. Intent to Continue Report

    This report serve to assist all level of ABOs to follow up with their downlines whose ABOship has expired: Previous month, Current month and due in next 2 months.

  4. Total

    ABO can view total number of ABOs expired or due for renewal in the next 2 months.

  5. Details

    ABO can view details of each ABO due for renewal.

  6. Expand ABO Details

    ABO can view more ABO details by clicking on the expand icon/arrow on the rightmost corner.

Business Report Page:
New ABO Activation Report
  1. Navigate Business Center

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of Business Center.

  2. Navigate New ABO Report Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections.

  3. New ABO Activation Report

    The purpose of this report is to let the ABO view the activations for their new ABOs.

  4. Sign Up Month

    ABO must select a sign up month from the drop down list to view details of ABO activations during that time.

  5. Search

    ABO can search for specific ABO details by filling in the field with ABO name or ID number and pressing enter.
