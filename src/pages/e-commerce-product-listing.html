Product Listing

How to access Product Listing Page (option 1)
  1. Shopping Tab

    ABO must hover over Shopping headline to expand categories below.

  2. Product Listing Page

    ABO must click on category icons below the navigation menu in the header to proceed to Category Landing Pages with products.

How to access Product Listing Page (option 2)
  1. Shop By Category

    ABO can access Product Listing Page by clicking on a category heading/icon to proceed to Category Landing Pages with products.

How to access Product Listing Page continued
  1. View All Category Products

    Clicking on ‘View All Products’ button at the bottom of the Category Landing Page will redirect ABO to the Product Listing Page where ABO can browse and shop products from the selected category.

Product Listing Page
Landing Page
  1. Filter Products

    ABO can filter products on the left hand side. ABO can Show All Products, Shop by Category, Shop by Brands filter or Shop by Needs

    1. Search Product

      ABO can search product list within the category by filling in the field and clicking enter.

    2. Sort Products

      ABO can sort products by selecting a filter from the drop down list.

    3. Show Products

      ABO can choose how many products to view per page by selecting from the drop down list.

    4. View Products

      ABO can choose to view the products in a list or a grid by clicking the icons on the right hand side.

    5. Products

      ABO must select quantity and click ‘Add to Cart’ button to add product to shopping cart. ABO can add a product to Favourite Shopping List by clicking the grey heart icon on the top right hand side of each product.

    6. Go To Page

      Entering a page number and clicking on the ‘Go’ button will allow the ABO to skip to a specific page in the product list.

    7. Bestsellers

      ABO can view best selling products in this section. Clicking on arrow icons on top right will allow ABO to see more products. ABO must select quantity and click ‘Add to Cart’ button to add product to shopping cart.
