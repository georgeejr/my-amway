Account Settings

How to Access Payment Details
  • Hover Over Header

    After logging in, ABO must hover over their name in the header until the drop down list appears with the different sections of My Account.

  • Payment Details

    ABO must click on ‘Preferences’ to proceed to the Payment Preferences section.

Preferences Page:
Landing
  • Navigate My Account Sections

    After logging in, ABO must hover over their name in the header until the drop down list appears with the different sections of My Account.

  • Default Display Page

    ABO can set which page to display as a Homepage after login.

  • Membership Renewal

    ABO can enable or disable the auto-renewal of their membership by sliding the toggle button from ‘Off’ to ‘On’.

  • AMAGRAM

    ABO can choose their ‘Preferred Language’ for their default website language after login and Amagram newsletter language.

  • Email Subscription

    ABO needs to check the box if they do not wish to receive emails from Amway regarding promotions and updates.

  • Save Changes

    Clicking on the ‘Save’ button will allow the ABO to save changes to their Preferences.

Step 1

How to Auto Renew Membership
  • Navigate My Account Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of My Account.

  • Membership Renewal

    ABO can enable or disable the auto-renewal of their membership by sliding the toggle button from ‘Off’ to ‘On’.

Step 2

How to Auto Renew Membership
  • Membership Renewal

    If ABO enables the auto-renewal by sliding the toggle button from ‘Off’ to ‘On’, they will be prompted to select a payment card to set up the auto-renewal.
