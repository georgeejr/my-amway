How to access Platinum Corner
  1. Access Platinum Corner

    ABO must click the section ‘Platinum Corner’ on the Knowledge Center landing page to proceed.

Platinum Activities & Events Page
eNewsgram
  1. Navigate Knowledge Center

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of Knowledge Center and product category information.

  2. Preview Content

    ABO can click on the title or image to enlarge and preview the exclusive Platinum eNewsgram.

  3. Navigate Activities & Events Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections.

  1. View Content

    ABO can choose to view the content in a list or a grid by clicking the icons on the right hand side.

  2. Download eNewsgram

    ABO can download the exclusive Platinum eNewsgram in a PDF file by clicking ‘Download’.
