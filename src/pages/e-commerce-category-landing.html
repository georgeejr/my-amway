Category Landing Page

How to access Category Landing Page
  1. Category Landing Page

    ABO must click on category icons below the navigation menu in the header to proceed to category landing pages with information about that category

Category Landing Page: 1
  1. Main Image Carousel

    ABO can view brand highlights or information about the selected category ie. Nutrition & Wellness.

  2. Shop By Category

    Clicking on a category heading/icon will redirect ABO to specific category page. Clicking on arrow icons on left and right will allow ABO to see more categories.

  3. Shop By Brand

    Clicking on a brand heading/logo will redirect ABO to specific brand page for selected category. Clicking on arrow icons on left and right will allow ABO to see more brands.

  4. Content

    ABO can view specific information about popular products/information related to the brand. Each tab will have an expandable content area.

  5. Content

    ABO can view specific information about popular products/information related to the brand. Clicking on the banner will redirect ABO to Brand Landing Page.

  6. Products

    ABO can view popular products for the selected category. Clicking on arrow icons on left and right will allow ABO to see more products. ABO must select quantity and click ‘Add to Cart’ button to add product to shopping cart.

  7. Highlights

    ABO can view category highlights in this section.

  8. Bestsellers

    ABO can view best selling products from the category in this section. Clicking on arrow icons on top right will allow ABO to see more products. ABO must select quantity and click ‘Add to Cart’ button to add product to shopping cart.

  9. View All Category Products-

    Clicking on ‘View All Products’ button will redirect ABO to the category listing page where ABO can browse and shop products from the selected category.
