Brand Landing

How to access Brand Landing
  1. Brand Landing Page

    ABO must click on category icons below the navigation menu in the header to proceed to category landing page.

How to access Brand Landing
  1. Shop By Brand

    ABO can access specific Brand Landing Page by clicking on a brand heading/logo. ABO can find relevant and important information about the brand on these pages.

Brand Landing Page
  1. Breadcrumb Navigation

    ABO can see their location in the website and navigation path hierarchy to reach this page.

  2. Brand Page Title

    This shows the ABO which brand page they are viewing.

  3. About the Brand

    ABO will be redirected to the external brand website by clicking ‘About The Brand’ button.

  4. Hero Banner Carousel

    ABO can view brand info on auto-rotation. Clicking on each image will redirect ABO to selected brand page.

  5. Content

    ABO can view specific information related to the brand. ABO can navigate tabs on left hand side for more information.

  6. Filters

    This section allows the ABO to filter products according to category for this brand.

    ***All’ will be selected by default and show all products from the brand. Search- ABO can search for specific products within the brand list. Sort By- ABO can sort products by selecting a filter from the drop down list. Show- ABO can choose how many products to view per page by selecting from the drop down list.

  7. Brand Products

    This section displays the products from a specific brand. ABO must select quantity and click ‘Add to Cart’ button to add product to shopping cart.

Brand Landing Page:
Grid View
  1. View Products

    ABO can choose to view the brand products in a list or a grid by clicking the icons on the right hand side.
