PV/BV Inquiry Screen

How to access PV/BV Inquiry
  1. View PV/BV Inquiry

    Clicking on 'View explanation on Volume type’ in the top right of the Business Tracker will redirect the ABO to the PV/BV Inquiry section of the Business Center.

  2. Our Brands

    ABO must click on ‘Our Brands’ to proceed to the Brand Zone section.

Business Center Page:
PV/BV Inquiry Screen
  1. Navigate Business Center

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of Business Center.

  2. Print/ Download

    ABO can 'Print' and 'Download' the page in report format by clicking these buttons in the top right.

  3. PV/BV Inquiry

    ABO can check the PV/BV for themselves or a downline ABO for the last year. To make a PV/BV inquiry, ABO must select a month from the drop down list, enter the chosen ABO number in the search field and click on the ‘View’ button.

  4. PV/BV Summary

    After making a PV/BV Inquiry, ABO can view a summary of the results in this section.

  5. Summary by Country

    ABO can view PV/BV Summary by Country by clicking on the expand icon/arrow on the rightmost corner.

  6. Business Performance History

    ABO can view Business Performance History by clicking on the expand icon/arrow on the rightmost corner.

  7. Downline Personal PV/BV

    ABO can view the PV/BV details of a downlines by clicking on the expand icon/arrow on the rightmost corner.

  8. Personal PV/BV Details

    ABO can view their PV/BV details by clicking on the expand icon/arrow on the rightmost corner.
