MyBiz

How to access MyBiz
  1. LOS Map

    Clicking on ‘LOS Map’ on the Dashboard will redirect the ABO to the MyBiz section of the Business Center.

How to log in to MyBiz
  1. Select Country

    ABO must select ‘Malaysia’ from the drop down list of countries.

  2. Login as an ABO

    ABO must log in to their account by filling in their ABO username and password and clicking on the ‘Log In‘ button. This will take the ABO into their MyBiz account

  3. Change Password

    Clicking on this link will redirect user to ‘Change Password’ where they can request for their details to be sent to them.

How to log out of MyBiz
  1. Logout

    ABO must click on ‘Log Out’ at the top right hand corner of the header to proceed to log out.

  2. Confirm Logout

    ABO must then click ‘OK’ on the pop up confirmation in order to logout of their MyBiz session.

  3. Change Password

    Clicking on this link will redirect user to ‘Change Password’ where they can request for their details to be sent to them.

How to navigate MyBiz
  1. Navigate MyBiz Sections

    Clicking on the icons in the navigation side bar will expand the menu to show the full titles. ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of MyBiz.

  2. Settings

    Clicking on the ‘Settings’ icon will redirect ABO to the Site Settings menu.

How to change settings
  1. Change Data Settings

    ABO can change site settings in this section. ABO has the option to Hide All Volume Data, Hide Inactive ABOs in group or Hide All ABOs with No Generated Volume by selecting or de-selecting the check boxes on the right.

  2. Language Settings

    ABO can change the Language settings of MyBiz here by selecting a language from the list available.

  3. Save Changes

    ABO must click the ‘Apply’ button in the top right hand corner of the navigation bar to save changes to Settings. ABO can cancel changes by clicking ‘Back’ on the left hand corner.
