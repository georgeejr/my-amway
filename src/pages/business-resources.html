Activities & Events Page
Monthly Promotions
  1. Navigate Knowledge Center

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections of Knowledge Center and product category information.

  2. Sort Videos

    ABO can sort information by date using the dropdown list.

  3. Navigate Activities & Events Sections

    ABO can click on the headings to access different sections.

  4. View Content

    ABO can choose to view the content in a list or a grid by clicking the icons on the right hand side.

  1. Preview Content

    ABO can click on the title or image to enlarge and preview the content about Monthly Promotions.

  2. Download Content

    ABO can download content in a PDF file by clicking ‘Download’.
