e-Commerce

How to access e-Commerce
  • Access e-Commerce

    ABO must click on ‘Shopping’ in the navigation bar header on the home page to proceed to the e-Commerce section.

E-Commerce Page:
Header Components
  • Access e-Commerce

    ABO must click on ‘Shopping’ in the navigation bar header on the home page to proceed to the e-Commerce section.

    • Search

      ABO can search the site product catalog, resource and knowledge center by filling in the field and clicking the search icon on the right hand side.

    • Navigate

      ABO can navigate to different sections of the website by clicking headings- Shopping Settings, Favourite List, My Cart, Message Center, My Account, Shopping Settings and Shop Finder.

  • Navigate Sections

    ABO can navigate to different sections of the website by clicking headings- Home Icon, Shopping, Business Center and Knowledge Center.

  • Quick Access Categories

    ABO can quick access specific categories by clicking on the icons- Nutrition and Wellness, Beauty, Personal Care, Home Living, Personal Shopper Catalogue.

  • Breadcrumb Navigation

    ABO can see their location in the website and navigation path hierarchy to reach this page.

  • Amway Logo Icon

    Clicking on the Amway Logo icon will redirect the ABO back to the Amway home page.

E-Commerce Page:
Shopping Tab
  • Shopping

    ABO can hover over Shopping headline to expand categories below.

E-Commerce Page:
My Favourite List
  • My Favourite List

    ABO must click the heading ‘My Favourite List’ to open overlay and show Shopping Lists. The shopping list currently set as favorite will have the check box selected against it. ABO can change the favourite by selecting checkbox next to preferred list.

  • Select Favourite List

    The shopping list currently set as favorite will have the check box selected against it. ABO can change the favourite by selecting the checkbox next to preferred list.

    ***Only one shopping list can be set as favourite at a time.

  • View All

    Clicking on 'View All' will redirect ABO to My Account> Shopping list section to view all Shopping Lists.

E-Commerce Page:
My Cart
  • My Cart

    ABO must click the heading ‘My Cart’ to be redirected to Shopping Cart section or ABO can hover over to open the mini shopping cart in overlay and view the total product count in the cart.

  • Free Delivery Tracker

    ABO can view how much they need to spend to qualify for Free shipping.

    ***This value is calculated in terms of percentage of completion.

  • Recently Added Items

    ABO can view last 3 recently added/ modified products from the shopping cart and Order Total with Order PV/BV.

E-Commerce Page:
Message Center
  • Message Center

    ABO must click the heading ‘Message Center’ to be redirected to Message Center section or ABO can hover over to open the mini message center in overlay.

  • Recent Messages

    ABO can view last 3 recently unread messages from the Message Center.

  • Messages

    Clicking on any message will redirect ABO to read the message selected.

  • View All

    Clicking on ‘View All' will redirect ABO to Message Center section.

E-Commerce Page:
Landing
  • Main Image Carousel

    ABO can view major promotions and announcements rotating. Clicking on arrow icons on left and right will allow ABO to move the banner images Previous/Next.

  • Latest News

    ABO can view latest news in this section. Clicking on an image will redirect ABO to that specific news page. Clicking on ‘View All’ will redirect ABO to the Latest News page.

  • Monthly Promotions

    ABO can view monthly promotions in this section. Clicking on arrow icons on top right will allow ABO to move the banner images Previous/Next.

  • Corporate Highlights

    ABO can view corporate highlights in this section. Clicking on ‘View All’ will redirect ABO to the Corporate Highlights page.

  • Digital Hotspot

    ABO can view digital hotspot in this section. Clicking on arrow icons on top right will allow ABO to move the banner images Previous/Next.

  • Welcome After Login

    ABO can select their Default Display Page i.e. Shopping or Business. ABO can see Message Center’s ‘Total Messages’ count. Clicking on ‘Read More’ button will redirect ABO to Message Center page.
    Shopping List – ABO can view the number of items in favorite shopping list. Clicking on this section will redirect ABO to Shopping List page. Shopping Cart -  ABO can view the number of items in shopping cart. . Clicking on this section will redirect ABO to Shopping Cart page.
