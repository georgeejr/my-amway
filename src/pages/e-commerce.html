How to access e-Commerce
  • Access e-Commerce

    ABO must click on ‘Shopping’ in the navigation bar header on the home page to proceed to the e-Commerce section.

E-Commerce Page:
Header Components
  • Access e-Commerce

    ABO must click on ‘Shopping’ in the navigation bar header on the home page to proceed to the e-Commerce section.

    • Search

      ABO can search the site product catalog, resource and knowledge center by filling in the field and clicking the search icon on the right hand side.

    • Navigate

      ABO can navigate to different sections of the website by clicking headings- Shopping Settings, Favourite List, My Cart, Message Center, My Account, Shopping Settings and Shop Finder.

  • Navigate Sections

    ABO can navigate to different sections of the website by clicking headings- Home Icon, Shopping, Business Center and Knowledge Center.

  • Quick Access Categories

    ABO can quick access specific categories by clicking on the icons- Nutrition and Wellness, Beauty, Personal Care, Home Living, Personal Shopper Catalogue.

  • Breadcrumb Navigation

    ABO can see their location in the website and navigation path hierarchy to reach this page.

  • Amway Logo Icon

    Clicking on the Amway Logo icon will redirect the ABO back to the Amway home page.
