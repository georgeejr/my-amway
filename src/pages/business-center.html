Business Center

How to access Business Center (before login)
  1. Access Business Center

    ABO must click the heading ‘My Cart’ in the header to open the mini shopping cart in overlay.

How to access Business Center (before login)
  1. Landing Page Before Login

    When the ABO is not logged in, this landing page appears after clicking on the Business tab in the navigation menu.

  2. Login

    ABO can log in to their account by filling in their username and password and clicking on the ‘Submit‘ button. This will take the ABO into their Business Dashboard.

  3. Start a Business Experience

    ABO can find information about starting a business in this section. ABO can click on the different headings to access more information.

  4. Business Support

    ABO can find information about how to get business support in this section. ABO can click on the different headings to access more information.

How to access Business Center (before login)
  1. Landing Page After Login

    When the ABO is already logged in, they are redirected to this Dashboard after clicking on the Business tab in the navigation menu.

  2. Dashboard

    ABO can reach the Dashboard by clicking on the ‘Dashboard’ icon at the top left of the quick links below the navigation menu in the header.

Business Center Page:
Quick Links
  1. Dashboard

    ABO can reach the Dashboard by clicking on the ‘Dashboard’ icon at the top left of the quick links.

    ***ABO must be logged in.

  2. PV/BV Enquiry

    Clicking on the ‘PV/BV Enquiry’ icon in the quick links bar will redirect the ABO to the PV/BV Enquiry section of the Business Center.

  3. LOS Map

    Clicking on the ‘LOS Map’ icon in the quick links bar will redirect the ABO to the LOS Map section of the Business Center.

  4. ITCCAS

    Clicking on the ‘ITCCAS’ icon in the quick links bar will redirect the ABO to the ITCCAS Group Renewal page of the Business Center.

  5. Business Report

    Clicking on the ‘Business Report’ icon at the top right of the quick links bar will redirect the ABO to the Business Report section of the Business Center.

Business Center Page:
Dashboard After Login (Normal)
  1. Business Tracker

    ABO can track their business progress and performance level in this section.

  2. View

    Clicking on 'View explanation on Volume type’ in the top right will redirect the ABO to the PV/BV Inquiry section of the Business Center.

  3. Birthday

    Clicking on 'View Birthday of the month’ in the top right will redirect the ABO to the MyBiz section of the Business Center.

  4. Business Report

    Clicking on reports will redirect the ABO to different report sections of the Business Center. Eg. Intent to continue report, Add new ABO report.

  5. LOS Map

    Clicking on ‘LOS Map’ will redirect the ABO to the MyBiz section of the Business Center.

  6. Order History

    Clicking on ‘Order History’ will redirect the ABO to the Order History section of My Account.

  7. Health & Beauty Assessment

    Clicking on ‘Health & Beauty Assessment’ will redirect the ABO to the Health & Beauty Assessment section.
