MyBiz Higher Award Tracking

How to access Higher Award Tracking
  1. Access Higher Award Tracking

    ABO must click on the menu icon at the top left hand corner to navigate the different MyBiz sections. ABO must click on ‘Higher Award Tracking’ to proceed to view Higher Award Tracking section.

    ***ABO will only see this section if they have one or more qualified Downlines.

Higher Award Tracking Page:
Landing
  1. Navigate MyBiz

    ABO can click on the icons to access different sections of MyBiz.

  2. Markets

    In this section the ABO can the Strongest Market, as well as their Primary Market.

    ***If the strongest is their primary, then they will only see one.

  3. Higher Award Tracking

    In this section the ABO can view an overview of all qualified Downlines and FAA points globally.

  4. Calendar Year

    ABO must click on the Calendar Year to choose which year they want to view.

  5. Monthly Breakdown

    ABO can view the monthly breakdown and qualification codes for each of their qualifying downlines.

  6. Help

    ABO can click ‘Help’ icon on right hand side for more information about Higher Award Tracking. This section will provide the ABO with a key for qualification codes and details.
