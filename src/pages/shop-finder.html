Shop Finder

How to access shop finder
  • Access Shop Finder

    Both non-logged in and logged in ABOs must click on the ‘Shop Finder’ link on the top right of the header to proceed to the Shop Finder section.

Shop finder page:
Landing
  • Search to Find a Product

    ABO can search for a specific product in this section.

    *** ABO cannot add the product to cart from Shop Finder section. Shop Finder is for viewing the product catalog and availability only.

  • Favourite Shop

    ABO can set a favourite shop by clicking the grey heart icon on the right hand side of each shop row.

    *** The shops set as favourites with red heart will always appear at the top of the shop list. ABO can remove a favourites shop by clicking the red heart icon.

  • Expand Details

    Clicking on the expand icon/arrow on the rightmost corner of each shop row will expand the shop specific details below.

Step 2

View Shop Details
  • Expand Details

    Clicking on the expand icon/arrow on the rightmost corner of each shop row will expand the shop specific details below

  • General Information

    ABO can view Shop Address, Shop Contact Number, Shop Manager, Shop Timings and there is a Link to Google Maps to find the Shop Location.

  • Promotions

    Clicking on the expand icon/arrow on the rightmost corner of the Promotions row will expand details of promotions running in shop below.

  • Events

    Clicking on the expand icon/arrow on the rightmost corner of the Events row will expand details of shop events below.

  • Product Catalog Section

    Clicking on the expand icon/arrow on the rightmost corner of the All Available Products row will expand the shop catalog with details below. 

Step 3

View Shop Catalog
  • Product Catalog Section

    Clicking on the expand icon/arrow on the rightmost corner of the All Available Products row will expand the shop catalog with details below. 

  • Filter Products by Category

    ABO must click on the headings to filter the shop product catalog by category.

  • Product Search

    ABO can search for a specific product by filling in the field and clicking the search icon.

  • Soft Products

    ABO can choose how to sort the products by clicking the arrow on the drop down list.

  • Show Products

    ABO can choose how many products to show per page by clicking the arrow on the drop down list.

  • View Products

    ABO can choose to view the products in a list or a grid by clicking the icons on the right hand side.
