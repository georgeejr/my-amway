Product Detail

How to access Product Detail Page (option 1)
  1. Search Product

    ABO can reach the Product Detail Page by searching the product name in the field and clicking enter on the Home Page search bar.

How to access Product Detail Page (option 2)
  1. Product Details

    Clicking on the product name in the Product Listing Page/ Category Landing Page/Brand Page or clicking the ‘View Details’ button on the product will redirect ABO to the specific Product Detail Page.

Product Listing Page
Landing
  1. Main Product Image

    ABO can mouse over to zoom-in and view the product image in an enlarged scale. ABO can also click on the image to enlarge it in a pop-up.

  2. Alternative Product Images

    ABO can mouse over to zoom-in and view the product image in an enlarged scale. ABO can also click on the selected image to view it in the Main Product Image section.

  3. Product Description

    *** ABO can view Product Description and see product variants in this section.

  4. Add to Cart

    ABO must select quantity and click the ‘Add to Cart’ button to add product to shopping cart.

  5. Product Information

    ABO can view detailed Product Information by clicking on the 3 key headings- Overview, More Information and FAQs.

  6. Product Information

    ABO can view detailed Product Information in this section.

  7. Other Products

    ABO can view other related product in this section. ABO must click on the product image to be redirected to the specific Product Detail Page.
